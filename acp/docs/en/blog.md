# Blog

<kbd>Backend</kbd> <kbd>Blog</kbd>

Here you can manage your blog. Create, edit or delete entries.
On the overview you will find all saved entries.

There are 5 different types of blog posts.

* Messages
* Images
* Galleries
* Video
* Link
* Download
