# Options

Options contain a title and at least two values.
When an option is assigned to a product, the customer can choose these options during the checkout process.

Example

| Title | Value 1 | Value 2 | ...  |
|-------|---------|---------|------|
| Color | red     | green   | etc. |


Options have no influence on the price of the product. If, for example, you want to offer a product
that is available in two options at different prices, it must be created as a variant.
